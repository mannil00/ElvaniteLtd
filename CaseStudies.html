Removal of Underground Reservior in Clacton

Elvanite Soil Remediation at work removing a concrete underground reservoir. The resulting concrete was recycled into MOT Type I and the void  backfilled when the reservoir was completely removed. The backfill consisted of clean clay material compacted in layers. CBR testing was carried out during the filling operation in order that it was compacted to MOT Highways Specification.
